Welcome to Stone-Age

We're a group of friends who all love Minecraft, and we've dedicated our lives to building a community around the game. We all have different backgrounds, but we all share one thing in common: our love for modded Minecraft. We want to make it easier for you to play with your friends or family members by giving you a platform where you can find people who share your interests.


We want this site to be a place where people can find new friends and enjoy themselves while playing Minecraft together. We want you to be able to build with your friends or family members without having to worry about what server or language they prefer or how many mods they have installed on their computer. If you're looking for a server where everyone gets along and has fun together, then come join us!
